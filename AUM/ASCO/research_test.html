Media and communication research investigates how information is created, shared, and interpreted across diverse platforms and audiences. It explores the evolving landscape of digital media, journalism, public discourse, and strategic communication, emphasizing the role of technology, culture, and society in shaping narratives. This field critically examines media ethics, audience behavior, and the impact of emerging tools like AI and immersive media. Complementing this, research in film, animation, and VFX delves into visual storytelling, cinematic techniques, and digital artistry. It fosters innovation in content creation, blending traditional methods with cutting-edge technologies such as virtual production and real-time rendering. Together, these domains promote interdisciplinary inquiry, empowering creators and scholars to craft compelling, inclusive, and socially relevant media experiences that resonate globally.
