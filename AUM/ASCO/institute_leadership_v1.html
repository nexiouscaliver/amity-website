“Storytelling shapes the world, and media holds the power to inform, influence, and inspire. Our mission is to cultivate creative storytellers who innovate, inspire, and drive meaningful change.”

The Amity School of Communication (ASCO) nurtures creative minds and future-ready media professionals. Through multidisciplinary programmes in journalism, filmmaking, digital media, and communication, students gain hands-on experience, industry exposure, and mentorship from seasoned professionals.

Our mission is to empower students to think critically, innovate, and craft impactful narratives. By blending theory with practical learning, ASCO equips communicators, storytellers, and content creators to make a meaningful contribution to the media landscape and society at large.

ASCO fosters a culture of creativity, experimentation, and ethical storytelling. With state-of-the-art facilities, industry collaborations, and experienced faculty, students are prepared to excel in traditional and emerging media platforms, shaping the future of communication with confidence and vision.
