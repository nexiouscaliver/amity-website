Well-equipped studios and editing labs

At Amity School of Communication (ASCO), our infrastructure is designed to mirror real-world media environments, giving students hands-on, industry-ready experience. From producing chat shows, short films, and advertising campaigns to creating podcasts, documentaries, and social awareness content, students learn by doing, using the same tools and workflows as professionals in the media industry.

Multimedia Lab (Animation & Design)
Multimedia Lab (Animation & Design)

The Multimedia Lab is a creative hub where students explore graphic design, animation, and digital media production. Equipped with 30 advanced systems and industry-standard software such as Adobe Creative Suite, MAYA, and AutoCAD, the lab provides students with practical exposure to designing for both print and digital platforms.

Video Editing Lab
Video Editing Lab

Our Editing Lab, featuring Macintosh systems and software such as Adobe Premiere and Final Cut Pro, enables students to master post-production workflows. Here, they develop technical and creative editing skills essential for careers in film, television, and OTT platforms.

Photography & Videography Studio
Photography & Videography Studio

The Photography Studio is a versatile space designed to support a wide range of creative productions, including product shoots, fashion photography, and professional videography. Equipped with advanced Canon and Nikon DSLRs, professional camcorders, and full-frame film cameras, the studio provides students with hands-on experience in using industry-standard tools. The inclusion of teleprompters and chroma setups enhances opportunities for video production, enabling learners to experiment with green screen techniques and on-camera presentations. With access to over 30 professional lights, students are able to explore diverse lighting setups, mastering both naturalistic and stylized illumination. These resources collectively allow them to refine essential skills in lighting, composition, and production, ensuring they can create high-quality visual content that meets professional standards. The studio thus serves as both a learning environment and a creative hub, preparing students for real-world practices in photography and media production. Beyond the shoot floor, a post-production suite with calibrated monitors and Adobe Creative Cloud supports color correction, retouching, and editing.

Film & TV Studio

ASCO's state-of-the-art Film and TV Studio is fully operational, equipped with professional lighting, acoustics, production control rooms, and chroma facilities. Students gain hands-on experience in film and television production, while the integrated news studio enables them to practice anchoring, producing, and reporting in a real newsroom environment, preparing them for careers in broadcast, film, and digital media.

Sound, Radio & Podcast Studio

Our sound and radio studios, along with dedicated podcasting spaces, provide students with practical training in voice modulation, sound editing, live broadcasting, and audio production. The Radio Room allows students to practice RJing, conduct interviews, and manage live shows, while the Sound Studio supports dubbing, mixing, and high-quality audio production.
