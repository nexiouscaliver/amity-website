Infrastructure - Amity School of Languages

Library

The library at ASL is well-stocked in the centralised library of AUM. Most of the reference material, magazines, journals, literary novels, and critical essays are made available to the students. It is specious so that students may sit and read there for hours.

Classrooms

ASL provides world class infrastructure and classrooms to the students. The process of Teaching-learning takes place in spacious, technically advanced and supportive environment that ensure complete concentration of the students and help achieve the learning outcomes

 

Language Lab

The University has a well-equipped language laboratory with latest software programmes available for improving language skills. All terminals are connected through a Local Area Network (LAN) to the main server of the University. Students also have access to uninterrupted wi-fi facility on the campus. The teachers support the students in using the lab for their benefits. It facilitates 30 students at a time with individual student booth to learn various languages like French, Spanish and German.
