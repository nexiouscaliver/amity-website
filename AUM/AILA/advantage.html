Advantage - Amity School of Languages
Advantages: Knowing a foreign language is an asset, as it offers a plethora of opportunities. In today’s hyper connected world and globalised economy potential employers consider this as a strength in an employee’s skill set.

Language learning in general also helps to develop strong cognitive skills, such as better concept formation, mental flexibility, multitasking, listening skills and problem-solving skills, in addition to improving social interaction and encouraging connection between peers.

  • Faculty mentoring and personal support for the academic and professional development of each student
  • Minor degree in the field of choice along with the major degree through a Minor Track Programme
  • Learning of any foreign language from the option French, German and Spanish upto A1 level
  • MOUs and Study Tours in the countries like France, Spain and Germany to get actual language learning experience by understanding native culture and accents

Advantages of Institute for eg. Startup cell, any skill development program, workshops, training etc.

Different competitions, workshops and language related activities are organized to strengthen the language and literature learning experience. Language Day is celebrated every year in the month of April.

Collaborations and trainings/ visits

ASL annually organizes study tour or student exchange programme with the universities in France, Germany and Spain. Students practice Foreign Language –French, German or Spanish in an immersive environment as they complete a Language Learning course for Forty hours, which improves their basics language acquisition skills. The respective universities award certificates after completion of the course which is an academic addition to CVs of the students. It opens doors for further academic and carrier opportunities and gives exposure to the students and makes them global in their outlook.

Choice Based Credit System (CBCS)

  • Through the CBCS students can choose courses of their own choice.
  • Students can learn at their own pace.
  • The CBCS makes further education in foreign universities easier with the transfer of credits.
  • The system brings uniformity in assessment of the performance of students.
