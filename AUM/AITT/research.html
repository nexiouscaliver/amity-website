The Amity Institute of Travel & Tourism (AITT) is committed to advancing research that shapes the future of tourism, hospitality, and allied sectors. Our faculty and scholars contribute to interdisciplinary domains with a strong focus on sustainable tourism, consumer behaviour, digital transformation, AI in tourism, heritage management, and rural/community-based tourism.

Our Expertise Areas

  • Tourism Planning and Sustainable Development
  • Airline Operations and Cargo Management
  • Travel Agency and Tour Operations
  • Tourism Entrepreneurship
  • MICE Tourism
