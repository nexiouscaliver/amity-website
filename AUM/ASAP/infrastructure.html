Computer Laboratory

Computer laboratory with state-of-the-art Hardware & software’s for Design , Modelling & Presentation. All registered software’s with Learning Video tutorials and a Lab assistant . Students are provided with Personalized AutoCAD Student versions for their Perusal.

Library

Well equipped Library with over 2000+ Reference books on Architecture, Design, Planning and Philosophy. Journals and Magazines in Hardcopy and Digital format are accessible to the students with a computerized track of Library stock.

Design Studios

Conditioned Design Studios complete with Drafting Boards, Lockers and Exhibit space for the Students. Studios designed to ensure ample Natural Light and Moving spaces enabling the Students to keep a lively and Creative growth conducive Learning Environment.

 

Model Making Room

A Model Making Workshop/ room for Display well equipped with Boards, Cutting platforms and ample space to stock and Model the imaginations of the students. AUM provides state of the art Software and Hardware for the 3D printing needs.

Surveying Laboratory

A surveying lab with Survey technology ranging from Table top surveys , Dumpy levels to the more advanced GPS integrated Total station. A survey lab with all the equipment’s and technicians for the same are provided whenever necessary.

Environmental Laboratory

Environmental lab is equipped with sophisticated Industry grade machinery to keep a track of all the Environmental checks. Light meters, Sound meters, Co2 analyzer , Temperature meters are few of the Equipment’s which are checked regularly and form an integral part of the AUM Green Campus.
