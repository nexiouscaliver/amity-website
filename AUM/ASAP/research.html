ASAP, being part of a research-driven university, is at the forefront of new research in architecture, planning, and interior design. Faculty and academics' research works present theoretical knowledge in critical areas of architecture as well as applications. Through such a rigorous study program, students obtain a critical grasp of the theoretical and practical methodologies utilised in doing architectural research, as well as new trends, major opportunities, and difficulties in the field. Research projects and publications allow faculty and researchers to consolidate their knowledge, skills, and practical applications.
