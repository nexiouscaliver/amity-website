The Amity School of Architecture and Planning (ASAP), founded in 2015, is a prominent institution that prepares students to address global challenges in development, innovation, and technology for the benefit of mankind. Dedicated industry specialists and experienced academics teach students. ASAP offers undergraduate architecture, planning, and interior design programs accredited by the COA.

ASAP's courses and research investigations incorporate all applicable theory into practice-based studies of human habitat, landscape, construction techniques, and global architectural style. The School encourages study on current challenges, industrial expertise, technology upgrades, critical analysis, and theoretical integration. The School contains a Construction Yard, Climatology Lab, Surveying Lab, Computer Centre, Material Lab, Exhibition Room, and Students Activity Area/Model Making Room for learning.

 

Expert academics and professionals make up ASAP's exceptional faculty. ASAP academics publish research papers in many journals. They advise students through research assignments and motivate them. Meritorious students receive university funding.

Mission

ASAP – AUM believes in over all growth of the students, as such, they study a few subjects more than the stipulated subjects in the Council of Architecture guidelines. Subjects like Environmental Science, Behavioral Science, English, Foreign Language and Minor track (for interior design students). These will prepare the student for the professional world in all respects and for post graduate studies in India or abroad.

Vision

ASAP – AUM has a vision of becoming the most prestigious college in India with an alumni that will become famous architects and interior designers nationally and internationally.
