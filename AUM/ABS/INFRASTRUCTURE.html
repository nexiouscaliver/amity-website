Infrastructure - CII School of Logistics

Amity University Mumbai with its future-ready, sprawling campus, a world - class learning infrastructure and over a kilometers long frontage on the Mumbai-Pune Expressway offers a 360-degree career transformational set-up. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure, the campus facilitates all-round growth of students. It also houses world class sports facilities for soccer, cricket, lawn tennis, and badminton. There are several well-appointed residential hostel options for students as well as faculty within its premises. The accommodation is "Apartment Style" with suites of bedrooms that share a common lounge and bathroom. Each bedroom provides double occupancy and so each apartment has 6 occupants. The well-appointed rooms are well lit and well ventilated, with geysers in every bathroom and TV set in the common room for entertainment. Each student is provided with a separate bed, mattress, pillow, pillow covers and sheets, cupboards, and study table with chair.

To make the environment safe, healthy and more conducive to study, the university ensures 24-hour water supply and power supply, tight security with surveillance cameras and 24X7 security guards. 24 hours medical room is also available for any medical need. Amity University Campus has been designed by a globally renowned US architect who has previously designed Boston and California State University.

The university main campus is also well-connected by university operated as well as public transport options from major nearby locations and just 10 km from Panvel station. It has been certified ‘Green’ and has a state–of–the–art ‘access-control’ systems to ensure utmost safety. 30 minutes’ drive from the proposed Navi Mumbai International Airport.

CII School of Logistics is located on the fourth floor of Main Building of Amity University Mumbai. It has Wi-Fi-Enabled spacious classrooms with Multimedia Projectors for Audio-Visual Learning. There is a huge seminar hall on the ground floor where various events, seminars and conferences are held from time to time. There is also a well-stocked library containing thousands of Management Books, Journals, Research Publications, etc.
