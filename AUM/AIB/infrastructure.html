Amity Institute of Biotechnology is a premier academic and research centre with state-of-art infrastructure and facilities. AIB has well equipped labs like Animal cell culture, Microbiology, Plant Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Cell biology, Environmental biotechnology, Bioprocess technology, Bioinformatics, Molecular biology and r-DNA technology labs that support innovative research.

Some of major high-end equipment such as HPLC, PCR machines, cooling centrifuges, spectrophotometers, deep freezers, Gel-Doc system, Gel electrophoresis systems for nucleic acids and proteins are available in the institutes laboratories. The institute has well-equipped research laboratories for research scholars to ensure uninterrupted operation of research activities.

All the classrooms are equipped with modern equipment’s for interactive teaching-learning process. We have a rich repository of books, journals, magazines research publications and references books to augment learning. AIB has classrooms, two tutorial rooms, ten lecture theatres, mini auditorium, conference room and modern labs.
