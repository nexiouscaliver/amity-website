Being a part of research driven University, AIB is at the forefront of innovative research in Biotechnology. The research studies undertaken by faculty and scholars present theoretical knowledge in vital areas of Biotechnology as well as its applications. Through such rigorous study program, students gain a critical understanding of theories and practical methods used in undertaking medical research, emerging trends, key opportunities and challenges involved in the commercialization of biotechnology. The research studies focus on the application of advanced biotechnology practice that sustain human and plant life. Faculty and scholars consolidate knowledge, skills, and practical applications through research projects and publications.
