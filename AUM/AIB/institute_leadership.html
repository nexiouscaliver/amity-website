“Science knows no country, because knowledge belongs to humanity, and is the torch which illuminates the world” - Louis Pasteur The Amity Institute of Biotechnology was established in 2014. On the academic platform, AIB offers cutting-edge cross-disciplinary graduate and postgraduate programmes in Biotechnology. The academic courses entail a range of multidisciplinary coursework, study, research and projects. AIB motivates students to conceptualise out-of-the-box ideas and novel concepts in the field of biotechnology.

The academic rigour at AIB provides an intellectual aura to students and researchers that contributes to knowledge acquisition and augments exploration of a gamut of innovative ideas that help in scientific endeavors. Our mission is to train the young minds and encourage them in research and development, to explore and invent innovative biomedical products that could transform medical science. Students harness knowledge and skills through basic and applied research. Research scholars work closely with experts in specialized areas of research and implement practical knowledge to cater to the needs of society in field of environment, energy, health and diseases.
